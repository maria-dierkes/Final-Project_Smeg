Technical Features

  • Crumb Tray

    There is a push release stainless steel crumb tray which is removable for emptying.

  • Multi-function Backlit buttons

    Buttons are used for stopping, reheating, and defrosting. Backlighting is used to see easily in the dark.

  • Sliding Leaver

    There is an easy-to-slide lever in the front of the toaster so user can push down bread with ease.

  • Anti-Slip Base

    Manufactured with rubber on the bottom of legs to make toaster not move while making a meal

Accessories

  • Sandwich Racks

    Sandwich racks are available to fit all toaster sizes to make tasty toasted sandwiches or to keep toast warm

  • Bun Warmer

    Bun warmers are available to fit all toaster sizes to heat through buns and pastries or to keep toast warm.
