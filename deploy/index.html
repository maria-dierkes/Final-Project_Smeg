When you fall in love with a toaster,
you'll find any excuse to use it! Get creative with
some toasted bagels, warmed croissants,
or even a toasted sandwich.
With 6 browning levels, and a bagel function,
SMEG toasters are built to accommodate
your tastes. The automatic pop-up system,
and a removable crumb tray
will make your life easier-than-ever.

Technical Features

Technical Features

Crumb Tray

There is a push release
stainless steel crumb tray
which is removable
for emptying.

Multi-function
Backlit buttons

Buttons are used for stopping,
reheating, and defrosting.
Backlighting is used to see
easily in the dark.

Sliding Leaver

There is an easy-to-slide
lever in the front of the
toaster so user can push
down bread with ease.

Anti-Slip Base

Manufactured with rubber
on the bottom of legs to
make toaster not move
while making a meal
